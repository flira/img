Brasil Quest

Monumenta, an communication and social strategy ad agency, identified a good opportunity of talking about tourism with foreigners using an unusual approach. Having in mind the ever growing demand for mobile content, the agency put all its chips on the creation of a game to reach foreigners of several ages. We were involved since the beginning of the project and we were responsible for everything from the creation of concepts, character design, plot, gameplay development and art direction to programming and development on both iOS and Android platforms.
